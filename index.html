    Is Move It available across Kenya? Je, Move It inapatikana nchi nzima?

    Yes! We serve Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Nakuru, Thika, and expanding monthly. Rural areas coming in 2026!

    Ndiyo! Tunahudumia Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Nakuru, Thika na tunapanuka kila mwezi. Maeneo ya vijijini yanakuja 2026!

    100% biodegradable jute packs • Solar-powered vans • We plant 1 tree per move • Average move saves 38kg CO₂!
